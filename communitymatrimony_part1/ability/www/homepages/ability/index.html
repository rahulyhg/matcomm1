Ability Matrimony is an exclusive matrimony site for singles with special needs or those wanting to marry someone with special needs.

Ability Matrimony will serve as an excellent platform connecting people with various types of special needs like physical impaired, mobility impaired, hemiplegic, spinal impairment, vision impaired, learning disabled, hearing impaired, quadriplegic, paraplegic and much more.

Ability Matrimony seeks to make a positive difference in the lives of people with special needs and empower them to find suitable life partners to form lifetime relationships.
