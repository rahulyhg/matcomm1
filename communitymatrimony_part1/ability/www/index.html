About Community Matrimony
Community Matrimony is a consortium of matrimony portals catering to various communities of Indian origin. Community Matrimony promotes the concept of marrying within the community as it is a proven fact that marriages within the community are happier and stable. Community Matrimony offers the pleasure of searching for a life partner within one's own community from across the globe at the click of a mouse.
